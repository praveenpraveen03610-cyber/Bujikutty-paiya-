One time calm ah idha padichu purinjuko... 🥺

Life la kashtam varathu normal da… aana kashtam vandhuchuna life mudinjudhu nu artham illa. 🌱 Ippo unakku ellam wrong ah nadakara madhiri feel aagalam… aana athu permanent illa. Romba periya success adicha people ellarum life la neraya thadava fail aagirukanga. Fail aana thaan namma strong aaguvom. 💪 Nee ippo feel pannura pain ellam oru naal un strength ah maari pogum. Un mela nambikkai vechavanga irukanga… naanum iruken. ❤️ Success konjam late ah varalam… aana kandippa varum. Nee try pannurathu stop pannadha. Oru naal nee nenaicha vida periya success achieve pannuva. 🌟

Bujjikutty… unakku eppovume naan iruken. Nee strong ah iru da… un life la kandippa periya success varum. 💖
